Projectify s a single-page, construction budget generating webapp.

Predict project costs by inputting square footage of the project area and applying $/SF rates.


User Instructions:

  • 1) Enter Square Footage and Budget into the form at the top right.
  • 2) Select applicable trades by clicking the grey buttons at the bottom of the page.
  • 3) If you remove a trade by accident, re-add it by clicking on the assoicated red button.
  • 4) Observe total projected cost and delta from your allowable budget at the top right.
  • 5) Click reset to start over.
